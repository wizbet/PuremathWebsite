Consultation Services

We specialize in all aspects of building and construction—residential, commercial, and industrial. From foundation to finish, our services include architectural design, structural works, renovations, interior fit-outs, plumbing, electrical installations, and roofing.... At Puremath, we combine quality materials, skilled craftsmanship, and innovative solutions to deliver durable, efficient, and visually stunning projects. Your vision, built to last

Professional services(Surveying, supervision,quantities & Estimates)

We offer professional services in land surveying, project supervision, quantity takeoffs,and cost esti-mation.Our team ensures precise measurements, accurate budgeting,and thorough oversight... throughout every phase of your project. With Puremath,you can count on clarity, accountability, and excellence in planning and execution—laying the groundwork for successful construction.

land acquisition and documentation (land verification,selling,buying and documentation processing)

We offer trusted land acquisition and documentation services to simplify your property journey. From land verification and due diligence to buying, selling, and processing legal documents,... we ensure every transaction is secure and transparent. With Puremath, you can invest with confidence—backed by expertise and integrity.

renting service (house rent accommodation,market stores etc.)

With Puremath, finding the right space is simple, secure, and stress-free.

skills development and training service

At Puremath Construction Works and Housing, we believe that skilled hands build stronger foundations. Our Skills Development and Training Service is dedicated to equipping individuals with the practical knowledge and technical expertise needed in today’s evolving... construction industry. From foundational skills to advanced techniques, we provide hands-on training tailored to industry standards—empowering workers, enhancing safety, and improving project quality across the board.

building drawing & design

We bring your ideas to life through innovative and functional building design. Our architectural drawing and design services cover residential,commercial, and industrial projects—tailored to meet your needs, budget, and style.... From concept sketches to detailed plans,Puremath ensures every line is drawn with precision, creativity, and purpose.

materials services, General building materials & Estimates (sand,stones,cement, iron rods blocks,water)

We supply and deliver quality general building materials including sand, stones, cement, iron rods, blocks, and water—everything you need to keep your project moving. ... Our team also provides accurate material estimates to help you plan efficiently and reduce waste. With Puremath, you get trusted materials, fair pricing, and dependable service every step of the way.

General construction works(roofing systems, wiring opp, plumbing,tiling,bio digester, painting, etc...)

Our services include durable roofing systems,... professional electrical wiring, reliable plumbing, precise tiling, eco-friendly bio-digester installations, and high-quality painting. With Puremath, every detail matters—delivering comfort, safety, and style in every project.
